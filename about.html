Our Story

Our Journey of Craftsmanship and Trust

Since 1993, Vijay Footwear has grown from a modest local store into a respected footwear destination known across Sangamner and Akole. Built on passion, dedication, and consistent quality, our brand represents decades of trusted service and customer satisfaction.

Over the years, we have continuously improved our collections, expanded our showrooms, and introduced premium brands to provide customers with footwear that meets modern fashion expectations without compromising on comfort.

Our Legacy

A Footwear Legacy Built on Quality

What began as a small family-run footwear store in Sangamner has evolved into a trusted multi-brand retail destination. The Wakchaure family has dedicated decades to understanding the footwear needs of local customers.

We believe that quality footwear is not a luxury — it is a necessity. That is why we stock brands that represent the perfect balance of design, comfort, and durability, ensuring every customer walks out completely satisfied.

Our showrooms are designed to offer a premium retail experience — well-organised, spacious, and staffed by knowledgeable professionals passionate about helping you find the right pair.

✨ Our new 5-storey premium showroom is currently under construction — a landmark destination engineered to deliver a truly world-class shopping experience, redefining luxury retail in Sangamner.

Our People

The Leadership Behind Our Legacy

Behind every successful footwear store stands a passionate team committed to excellence, customer care, and continuous growth.

Satish Wakchaure

Founder & Visionary

The visionary behind Vijay Footwear, Satish ji established the store in 1993 with a dream of bringing quality footwear to Sangamner. His passion for craftsmanship and customer service laid the foundation of everything we are today.

Niraj Wakchaure

Proprietor & Strategic Leader

Carrying forward the family legacy, Niraj leads the business with sharp strategic vision. His focus on brand selection, customer relationships, and operational excellence has driven Vijay Footwear's growth and expansion.

Sparsh Wakchaure leads operations and digital media, serving as the digital voice of Vijay Footwear. He drives the brand's online presence through innovative strategies and modern storytelling.

Behind every successful customer experience stands a dedicated team that ensures smooth operations, well-organised inventory, accurate billing, and personalised assistance for every visitor who walks through our doors.
